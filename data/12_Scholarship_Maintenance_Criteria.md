## 1. Purpose
This document outlines the criteria required for students to maintain their merit-based scholarships and financial aid support throughout their academic tenure.

## 2. Scope
This document applies to all full-time undergraduate students at VinUniversity who receive merit-based scholarships or financial aid. It details the requirements for maintaining these awards.
- Merit-based Scholarships: Entry merit-based scholarships are awarded for the entire period of study and are not eligible for scholarship-level upgrades.
- Need-based Financial Aid: Entry need-based financial aid is awarded for the entire duration of study. Refer to the current guidelines for financial aid application: [Guidelines for Student Financial Support Request](https://policy.vinuni.edu.vn/all-policies/guidelines-for-student-financial-support-request/)
- Additional Financial Support: Students may apply for additional financial support from other sources. This will follow the guidelines or criteria specific to each type of support.
[Guidelines for Student Financial Support Request](https://policy.vinuni.edu.vn/all-policies/guidelines-for-student-financial-support-request/)

## 3. Criteria to maintain the entry merit-based scholarship and financial aid support
1.      Maintaining a cumulative GPA of 3.2 or higher in the academic year under evaluation (calculated as the average of the two main semesters: Fall and Spring).
2.      Maintain good disciplinary standing: The student must not commit major misconduct (Tier 3) or extremely serious misconduct (Tier 4) as per [Student Code of Conduct](https://policy.vinuni.edu.vn/all-policies/student-affairs-regulations-code-of-conduct/) and [Student Academic Integrity](https://policy.vinuni.edu.vn/all-policies/student-academic-integrity/).
3.      Complete the E.X.C.E.L self-evaluation and meet with the Advisor to discuss the self-evaluation.
(a)   Automatic Downgrade (1 level – based on current scholarship scheme of the academic year) – Applied if the Academic Year GPA is between 0.0 – 2.49 (below good academic standing).
(b)  Maintain the scholarship with the condition – Applied if the Academic Year GPA is between 2.50 – 3.19 (from good academic standing and above), applicable for Full and 100% scholarships.
The students will be granted an additional regular semester to improve their academic and E.X.C.E.L performance. The GPA and E.X.C.E.L performance at the point of re-assessment will be used for further consideration.
Note: Special cases may be reviewed by the Committee for consideration.
